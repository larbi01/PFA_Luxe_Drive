About

Experience the luxury of the road with Luxe Drive

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    LUXE DRIVE started in 2009 as a small car rental business with just a few cars. Despite the challenges of starting a new business, the team was committed to providing high-quality service at affordable prices. Over the next few years, LUXE DRIVE gained a reputation for excellence, and customers began to flock to the company for their rental needs.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    In March 2011, LUXE DRIVE officially became an agency, with a wider range of vehicles and more services available to customers.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    As the years went on, LUXE DRIVE continued to grow and expand. In December 2015, the company transitioned to a full-service model, offering everything from basic rentals to customized packages for special occasions. With a focus on customer satisfaction, LUXE DRIVE quickly became one of the most popular car rental agencies in the area.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    In July 2020, LUXE DRIVE embarked on a new phase of expansion, introducing new vehicles and services to meet the needs of an ever-growing customer base. With a commitment to quality, affordability, and excellent customer service, LUXE DRIVE is proud to be one of the leading car rental agencies in the region, serving customers with the same dedication and passion as they did during their humble beginnings.
